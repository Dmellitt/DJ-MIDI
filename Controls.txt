- Hardware from the left -

Buzzer: Plays all sound

Dial 1: Switch between five modes
  Mode 1: Play fairy fountain theme
  Mode 2: Play Kingdom Hearts title theme
  Mode 3: Play Mario underwater theme
  Mode 4: Play user input notes from C scale
  Mode 5: Play user input notes from a continuous slide
  
Dial 2: Set the note to play in modes 4 and 5

Button: Turn on the note in modes 4 and 5
